PAWS Adoption Process

These are the steps to adopting a pet according to the PAWS website:

Submit the adoption application form
Attend the zoom interview
Meet the shelter animal in person
Visit your chosen pet to confirm your choice
Wait for vet clearance and scheduled pick up
Pay the adoption fee of ₱500 (Cat) / ₱1000 (Dog)
Take your pet home!
